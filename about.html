ABOUT US

Powering Industries,
Building Trust

Falcon Energy Group is a global petroleum and energy solutions company committed to innovation, sustainability, and long-term industrial growth.

As a proud subsidiary of Falcon Aerospace Pvt. Ltd., India, we specialize in oil, gas, and petrochemical trading, project funding, and logistics across Russia and worldwide markets. Our goal is to connect industries through responsible energy management, ensuring transparency, efficiency, and measurable value in every project. Over the years, Falcon has become a dependable name in global trade — providing strategic expertise in sourcing, refining, and logistics while maintaining environmental and operational integrity.
